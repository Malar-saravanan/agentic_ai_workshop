=========================================
SHOPWISE ELECTRONICS — STORE POLICIES
=========================================

--- RETURN POLICY ---
All products can be returned within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Items must be in original packaging and unused condition.
Opened electronics are subject to a 15% restocking fee.
Defective items can be returned at no cost within 90 days.
Digital products and gift cards are non-refundable.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
Return shipping is free for defective items; customers pay return shipping for change-of-mind returns.

--- WARRANTY TERMS ---
All electronics come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects.
Extended warranty plans are available at checkout:
  - 2-Year Plan: Adds 1 extra year of coverage for 10% of product price.
  - 3-Year Plan: Adds 2 extra years of coverage for 18% of product price.
Warranty covers hardware failures, battery defects, and manufacturing issues.
Warranty does NOT cover accidental damage, water damage, or unauthorized repairs.
To claim warranty, contact support with your order number and proof of purchase.

--- SHIPPING OPTIONS ---
Standard Shipping (5-7 business days): Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99.
Express Shipping (2-3 business days): $12.99 for all orders.
Next-Day Shipping (1 business day): $24.99, available for in-stock items only.
Free shipping applies before taxes and after discounts.
Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same day.
We ship to all 50 US states. International shipping is not currently available.

--- LOYALTY PROGRAM (ShopWise Rewards) ---
Earn 1 point for every $1 spent on purchases.
Points can be redeemed for discounts: 100 points = $5 off.
Loyalty Tiers:
  - Bronze (0-499 points): Standard benefits, earn 1x points.
  - Silver (500-1499 points): Free standard shipping on all orders, earn 1.5x points.
  - Gold (1500+ points): Free express shipping, earn 2x points, early access to sales.
Members get a $10 birthday coupon every year.
Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity.

--- PRICE-MATCH GUARANTEE ---
We match prices from major online retailers (Amazon, Best Buy, Walmart).
Price match requests must be submitted within 14 days of purchase.
The competing price must be for the identical item (same model, color, condition).
Price matching does not apply to marketplace sellers, auction sites, or flash sales.
If approved, we refund the difference plus an extra 5% of the difference as store credit.
